Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I register for the bus service?

Registration for the bus service can be done during the college admission process. Existing students can contact the administrative office for more information.

2. What should I do if I miss the bus?

If you miss the bus, you can either arrange for your own transportation or catch the bus at the next boarding point, provided it is on the same route.

3. Are there any rules for bus conduct?

Yes, students are expected to maintain discipline and follow the bus rules and regulations to ensure safety and comfort for all passengers.
